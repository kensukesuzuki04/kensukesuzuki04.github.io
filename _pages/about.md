I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at [Clark University](https://www.clarku.edu).
My research focuses on **international trade and spatial economics**, with recent work on
the labor-market and regional implications of foreign employment in Japan.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the Pennsylvania State University.

You can find my work on the [Publications](/publications/) page and download my
[CV](/files/cv.pdf). Teaching materials, course slides, and data &amp; AI resources are
collected on my [Teaching page](https://kensukesuzuki04.github.io/teaching/).
